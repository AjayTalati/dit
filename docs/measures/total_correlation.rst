Total Correlation
=================

The total correlation :cite:`Watanabe1960`, also known as the multi-information
or integration, the total correlation is another generalization of the mutual
information. It is defined as:

.. math::
   \T[X_{0:n}] = \sum \H[X_i] - \H[X_{0:n}]
